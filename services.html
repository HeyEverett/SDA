What are Services?

Also referred to as Waiver Services, these are assistances provided to families with a child with a physical or intellectual disability. The benefits and types of services range depending on the independence of the individual, and the amount of care needed.

There is an application process that must take place, where need is assessed and your eligibility for these services is determined.There are a few different kinds of waiver services, and while we cannot administer these waivers, if you need assistance in the appplication process, we can help you get started.
