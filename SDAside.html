How to Access Services

Intellecutal Disabled (ID)Waiver Services Contact the ID Case Management Program at the Community Services Board that serves your locality. To locate the Community Services Board that serves your locality or learn about the different types of ID Waiver services visit the Department of Behavioral Health and Developmental Services website at www.dbhds.virginia.gov. Click on "Locate Services in VA" under the menu section. Consumer Directed Personal Attendant Services Contact your local Health Department and request a screening for services. To locate your local Health Department or learn about other services under the Elderly and Disabled Waiver visit the Virginia Health Department website at www.vdh.state.va.us. Click on local health districts.
